    Ohm Sweet Ohm

    Ohm Sweet Ohm is a smart home monitoring system for recreational vehicles and campers that actively records water pressure, propane pressure, wastewater level, and air quality within the RV or camper. The final aspect of Ohm Sweet Ohm is its ability to toggle the lights within the camper and extending or retracting the awning.

    Study Rogue

    Study Rogue is a Roguelike game that integrates the fun filled aspects of a videogame with the educational aspects of studying flashcards. The aim of this project is to assist those who tend to hyperfocus while playing games and trick their brain into learning while playing an enjoyable game. This game is currently a work in progress but new work is being added to it daily.
